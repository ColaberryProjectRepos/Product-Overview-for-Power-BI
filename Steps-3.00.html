What is Power BI embedded? 3

Microsoft Power BI Embedded allows application developers to embed stunning, fully interactive reports and dashboards into apps without the time and expense of having to build your own controls from the ground-up.

With Power BI Embedded you can embed and supercharge your applications with stunning, fully interactive visuals, dashboards and reports into your applications.


a.    Share and collaborate on the go.


Easily annotate reports with your touch-enabled screen, to draw your team’s focus to new insights. Share live reports and dashboards directly from the app to keep your team on the same page.


b.    Stay in the know.


Get push notifications for personal data alerts to your device and use 3D touch on iOS to quickly access shortcuts directly from your home screen. See your data update in real time on phones, tablets, and Apple Watch.



c.    Flexible and secure mobile access.

You can author for mobile users with Power BI Desktop and the Power BI service. Securely access Power BI reports and dashboards, SQL Server Reporting Services, and your on-premises Power BI Report Server content—all in one app. Keep your company’s data secure with Microsoft Intune mobile device and application management capabilities.


The Power BI service (SaaS) and the Power BI Embedded Service in Azure (PaaS) have APIs for embedding your dashboards and reports. This means you will have a set of capabilities and access to the latest Power BI features - such as dashboards, gateways and app workspaces - when embedding your content.
