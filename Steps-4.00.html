What are Power BI visualization features? 4

Power BI helps in enhancing your stories driven by data, enables you to explore, visualize and share data in a creative, compelling and interactive ways that engage and allows you to dig deep into stories that are relevant to users.

Develop custom visuals

·         Custom visuals allow you to create your own visuals for use within Power BI reports. Custom visuals are written in TypeScript which is a superset of JavaScript and Timeline story teller.

·         Great import capabilities and great drag and drop features.

-          Easily add custom visuals into Power BI

-          Developed by the community and Microsoft

-          Easily consumed through Power BI Desktop
